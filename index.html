"Hong Ye Ti Shi" is the name given by fans to the CP of Chinese singers Lars Huang and Sky Shi. The two became acquainted in 2018 through their participation in the Hunan TV music program Super-Vocal (Sheng Ru Ren Xin), and their close interactions on the show made them popular among fans. As the youngest members of the program, fans affectionately referred to them as the “Yao’er Duo” (meaning “youngest duo”). After Super-Vocal ended, Lars Huang chose to continue his studies at Berklee College of Music, while Shi Kai stayed in China to attend university. During his university years, Sky Shi participated in a variety show called The Great Detective Academy (Ming Zhen Tan Xue Yuan), where he made many new friends.

After Lars Huang returned to China, he became a regular cast member on Great Escape Season 4 and Detective College Seasons 6 and 7. The two were reunited once again.

Lars Huang (born April 21, 1999） is a Chinese pop-classical crossover singer and actor. He was born in Lanzhou, Gansu Province， and raised in Chengdu, Sichuan. He is currently studying Contemporary Music Production at the Berklee College of Music.

Sky Shi (born December 14, 1999) is a Chinese baritone singer and a graduate of the Vocal Music Department of Sichuan Normal University. In 2018, he participated in the first season of Hunan TV’s bel canto music show "Super–Vocal", where he was the youngest performer of the season. In 2019, he joined Mango TV’s variety program "The Detectives’ Adventures" (Ming Zhen Tan Xue Yuan), once again becoming the youngest cast member on the show.

In November 2018, Sky Shi made his official debut as a performing member on Hunan TV’s vocal performance show "Super–Vocal Season 1". In March 2019, he released his first digital single, "The Elk King", marking a step outside his classical bel canto style with a bright and upbeat melody that resonated with youthful energy.

In November 2019, he joined the reality game show "The Detectives’ Adventures: Season 1" as an “academy trainee,” later becoming a regular cast member. On June 3, 2020, he co-wrote and performed the show’s theme song, "NO.1 — Nan Bo Wan". On July 29, he participated in the real-life escape room reality show "Great Escape: Elite Edition Season 2", and eventually became a regular on the program.

In 2022, he released a series of singles including "Exclusive Listener", "YOU", "Finale Performance", and "Not Tomorrow, But Now". In 2023, he followed up with tracks like "Thanks", "Blank", "Forever Passionate", and "Romance", and launched his “Project K” solo concert tour in September.

In April 2024, he released his first physical album, "Counting Stars All Night". In June, he kicked off his second solo tour, titled "Blooming Along the Way". In August, he participated in the fourth season of the music competition show "Call Me by Fire". In September, he received the “Weibo Rising Musician” award at the 2024 Weibo Music Awards. In December, he appeared in the drama series "Thanks, Class Three".
